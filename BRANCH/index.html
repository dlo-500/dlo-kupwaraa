About the Office

District Litigation Office, Kupwara

The District Litigation Office (DLO) Kupwara operates under the Department of Law, Justice & Parliamentary Affairs, Government of Jammu & Kashmir. We provide legal representation and litigation management on behalf of government departments across all courts in Kupwara district.

Our office coordinates with departments including Revenue, Labour, Forest, Education, PWD, Police, and others to ensure robust legal defence and timely compliance with court orders.

⚖️

Legal Representation

Government counsel in civil, service and labour matters across all courts.

Case Management
